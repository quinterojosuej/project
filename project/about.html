About the Creators:

The Creators: Gustavo Aquino - Jonathan Quintero - Jesus Caballero - Noah Ahmed

all noticed a big problem with new incoming students. The problem they noticed was that no one knew where anything was in their new area. Examples were nearby stores, malls, restaurants, coffee shops, events, activites, etc. WhatToDo shows all that. What makes WhatToDo different from traditional maps is that this shows everything within a certain radius and will show events that are happening locally.
